Our History

Our History

2022
2022

The Foundation Year

Founded in 2022, Clear IT Solutions began as a dynamic US-based IT staffing and recruiting firm with a dual presence in Delaware, USA, and Hyderabad, India. From day one, our vision was clear: to empower businesses with top-tier tech talent while equipping professionals with the skills and opportunities needed to thrive in the digital age.

Building a Global IT Solutions Partner

What started as a staffing firm quickly evolved into a multifaceted IT solutions provider, offering:

- IT Staffing & Recruitment – Bridging talent gaps for startups to Fortune 500 companies. - Technology Training & Real-Time Projects – Hands-on training in cutting-edge technologies. - Internships & Certifications* – Launching careers with industry-recognized credentials.

2023
2023

Expansion into Training & Workforce Development

Launched a comprehensive IT training division, offering industry-relevant courses in Full Stack Development, Data Science, Cloud Computing, DevOps, and more.
Introduced real-time project experience and mentorship programs, helping professionals gain practical, hands-on exposure. Started internship and certification initiatives, creating career-launching opportunities for college students and job seekers. Gained traction with startups and mid-size tech firms seeking pre-trained, job-ready candidates.
2024
2024

Strategic Global Collaborations & Recognition

Signed key partnerships with multinational companies like IBM, TCS, Infosys, and others for staffing and workforce solutions. Delivered corporate training programs for enterprise clients, helping teams upskill in cutting-edge technologies. Scaled operations across multiple verticals including healthcare, finance, logistics, and retail. Recognized as a trusted name in global talent sourcing and IT workforce development. Reached the milestone of training 5,000+ professionals* and *successfully placing hundreds in high-impact roles.
